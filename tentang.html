Company Profile

PT AGRI PRIMA INDOTAMA was established in Batam, and PT Agri Prima Indotama in Medan, North Sumatera, has been experienced in supporting the Palm Oil Industry since 1993, Automation and Instruments Control, Electrical Parts, Filter processes, Rubbers, Gaskets, mechanical seals, Pumps & Accessories, all kinds of valves, and Specialty Chemicals.

We are specialist also in:

  1. Plate Heat Exchanger, we offer competitive engineering alternatives and solutions for our customers.
  2. Niagara Filter For Refinery Palm Oil (Leaves, Remeshing Leaves, Linier Sheet etc).
  3. Chiller, we offer services and maintenances for ABS Chillers and Electric Chillers.
  4. Clean Cooling Tower and Dirty Cooling Tower for Cleaning and Service also we can supply part of the cooling tower.
  5. Boiler Cleaning and Service also can supply part of Boiler.
  6. Mechanical, Electrical and Civil Works.

We have close cooperation with worldwide companies from Europe, USA, Japan, China, Taiwan and Singapore, representing well-known manufacturers with a wide range of spare parts applications. We are also a distributor of chemicals which encompasses the Water Service segment, focusing on innovative treatment of Boiler Water, Cooling Water, Reverse Osmosis, and Waste Water for customers across various industrial and institutional markets, PAY-OFF chemical PET ROFER coolant for milling, cutting, and machining. We provide integrated solutions to cleaning and pollution problems, and support for maintenance requirements, which are tailored to the needs of our clients to optimize production efficiency, reduce long-term operating costs, and create a cleaner working environment.
